About Gnosis

Gnosis makes unstructured data searchable without manual tagging. It watches your files, understands their content, and builds a semantic knowledge base — all locally.

How it Works

Upload files or drop them into the watched directory. Gnosis automatically extracts text from PDFs, Word documents, presentations, and plain text. Images are analysed by a local LLM to generate rich descriptions. All content is chunked, embedded, and indexed. When you search, your query is compared against every chunk using cosine similarity — returning the most relevant results instantly.

🔍

Semantic Search

Find content by meaning, not just keywords. Natural language queries across all your documents.

👁️

Image Understanding

Local LLM generates rich descriptions for images, making visual content fully searchable.

📂

Multi-Format Support

PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, JPG, PNG — all automatically parsed, chunked, and indexed.

⚡

Real-Time Monitoring

Watchdog-based file monitor tracks creates, updates, deletes, and renames in real time.

🔒

Local-First

Your data never leaves your machine. All processing and indexing happens on your own hardware.

🧩

Chunk-Based Retrieval

Documents are split into overlapping chunks for precise, granular search results.
